Privacy Policy for Animix

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

Animix ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Animix mobile application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, seller verification videos
  • Listing Data: Pet details, images, location
  • Payment Data: Transactions via secure third-party providers
  • Usage Data: App activity, search history, interactions

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • Provide and improve services
  • Verify users and prevent fraud
  • Enable buying/selling transactions
  • Communicate updates and notifications
  • Ensure platform safety and compliance

3. DATA SHARING

  • Buyer and seller information is shared to complete transactions
  • Payments handled securely via third-party providers
  • We do NOT sell user data
  • Data may be shared if required by law

4. ADMIN ACCESS & MONITORING

Animix includes an internal admin system to monitor platform activity for safety and fraud prevention.

  • Admins can view user profiles, listings, and transaction records
  • Admins may review uploaded documents for verification
  • Admins can suspend or delete accounts violating policies
  • This access is strictly used for security and support purposes

5. DATA SECURITY

We use secure technologies and industry standards to protect user data.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Access or update your data
  • Request account deletion
  • Opt-out of marketing communication

7. DATA RETENTION

User data is retained while the account is active. Deleted accounts are removed within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal purposes.

8. PAYMENTS AND ADS

  • Physical services → Razorpay
  • Digital purchases → Google Play Billing
  • Ads → Google AdMob (may collect Advertising ID)

9. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

We use trusted services like Razorpay, Google Play, and OTP providers. These services have their own privacy policies.

10. CHILDREN POLICY

Animix is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

11. PERMISSIONS

  • Storage → Upload media files

12. GOOGLE PLAY COMPLIANCE
